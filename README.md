# ImmigrantTax.ai — Cross-Border Tax Analyzer

**AI-powered cross-border tax analysis for 15+ countries.** Check tax residency, find treaty benefits, detect FBAR/FATCA/T1135 filing obligations — all free.

🔗 **Live:** [https://atalovesyou.github.io/immigranttax-ai/](https://atalovesyou.github.io/immigranttax-ai/)

## The Problem

Cross-border tax is a nightmare. Immigrants, expats, digital nomads, and remote workers face:
- Multiple tax residency rules (183-day tests, substantial presence, domicile)
- Double taxation risk without treaty knowledge
- Hidden filing obligations (FBAR, FATCA, T1135, Schedule FA)
- CPA fees of $500-3,000 per country pair

## Features

- **15+ Country Tax Database** — Real rules, forms, deadlines, penalties for major immigrant corridors
- **22+ Bilateral Tax Treaties** — Withholding rates, residency tie-breakers, pension/Social Security provisions
- **8-Step Guided Analyzer** — Walk through your situation, get risk-scored findings
- **US Substantial Presence Test** — Auto-calculate days and exemptions
- **183-Day Residency Tests** — For 10+ countries
- **FBAR/FATCA/T1135 Detection** — Threshold monitoring and filing reminders
- **Treaty Lookup Tool** — Quick country-pair treaty reference
- **Cost Savings Calculator** — Estimate savings from treaty benefits
- **Professional PDF Export** — Tax situation summary for your CPA
- **100% Client-Side** — Your financial data never leaves your browser
